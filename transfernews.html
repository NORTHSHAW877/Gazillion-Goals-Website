LATEST TRANSFER NEWS

Trent Alexander-Arnold

The English right back has left Liverpool and joined Spanish giants Real Madrid.

He departs Anfield after 354 appearances and 23 goals for the club.

Florian Wirtz

On 20 June 2025, Florian Wirtz joined Premier League club Liverpool.

It is rumored he signed a long-term contract for £100 million, becoming the most expensive transfer in the history of the Bundesliga (replacing Ousmane Dembélé as record holder).

Tijjani Reijnders

On 11 June 2025, Premier League club Manchester City announced the signing of Reijnders on a five-year deal reportedly worth £46.5m.

The 26-year-old midfielder joined from AC Milan, where he made 104 appearances and scored 19 goals in two seasons.

Jobe Bellingham

Jobe Bellingham joined Borussia Dortmund from Sunderland on a five-year contract until June 2030.

Sunderland say the fee is a club record, believed to be 32m euros (£26.96m) plus 5m euros (£4.2m) in add-ons.

The 19-year-old midfielder has played for the Black Cats since 2023, making 90 appearances, and helped them secure promotion to the Premier League last season.

Paul Pogba

AS Monaco has officially announced the signing of Paul Pogba until 2027.

The Former Juventus and Manchester United player spent the last few months as a free agent following an 18 month doping ban.

The 32 year old midfielder is determined to relaunch his career with the 2026 World Cup already in sight.
